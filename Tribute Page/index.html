Here's a time-line of Sushma Swaraj's life:

  • 1952: Sushma Swaraj was born on 14 February 1952 at Ambala Cantonment, Haryana.
  • 1977: Elected as Member, Haryana Legislative Assembly.
  • 1987: Cabinet Minister, Labour and Employment, Government of Haryana.
  • 1991: Member of Rajya Sabha
  • 1996: Union Cabinet Minister, Information and Broadcasting.
  • 2000: Member, Rajya Sabha (4th term)
  • 2003: Minister of Health & Family Welfare
  • 2006: Member, Rajya Sabha (5th term)
  • 2009: Deputy Leader of the Opposition in the Lok Sabha.
  • 2014: Member, 16th Lok Sabha (7th term).
  • 2019: Dies at the age of 67
